We will concern ourselves with the following SDE:
\begin{align*}
	 dX_t = A_t X_t dW_t
\end{align*}
with
\begin{align*}
A_t=
\left[
\begin{array}[c]{cc}
f^{11}(t) & f^{12}(t)\\
0 & f^{22}(t)
\end{array}
\right]
\end{align*}
Here we chose:\begin{align*}
f^{11}(t)\equiv 2,
f^{12}(t)= t,
f^{22}(t)\equiv -1,
\end{align*}
